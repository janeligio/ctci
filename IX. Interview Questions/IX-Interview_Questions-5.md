## Bit Manipulation By Hand
(row by row)
1. 0110 + 0010 = 1000
2. 0011 * 0101 = 1110
3. 0110 + 0110 = 1100
4. 0011 + 0010 = 0101
5. 0011 * 0011 = 1001
6. 0100 * 0011 = 1100
7. 0110 - 0011 = 0011
8. 1101 >> 2 = 0011
9. 1101 ^ (~1101) = 1111
10. 1000 - 0110 = 0010
11. 1101 ^ 0101 = 1000
12. 1011 & (~0 << 2) = 1000
